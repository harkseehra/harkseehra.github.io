> ## Life is all about living simply in a simple world.

Life is all about simplicity. As simple as it sounds, it is the beauty of thinking more simply. Sometimes, we approach it emotionally, but other times rationally. The more you give thought to it, the more complexity webs the mind, as there are now 100,000 pathways of information connecting with 100,000 possibilities. While some gain and learn for attention, only the things that are simple and to the heart make sense, bringing light to the mind. 🌟💡❤️

> ## Like, you scroll Instagram to watch 100,000 clips each year at least, and what do you learn from it? 🤔

Well, there's a reward for the influencers to entertain you, while some producers make and create art. But as you're standing in different shoes—it's not your Nike Air Jordans but more like the work-comfy shoes you wear daily—that marks the line between a millionaire with a growth mindset and a 20-year-old with no job, all fake chains, and expensive boots he paid for with a credit card. 💳👟

Now, this young person works a job to afford an extraordinary, inflatable lifestyle, while the millionaire understands the importance of a daily routine and activities, putting quality into that time. On the contrary, wannabes dress up on weekends and post weekend vibes with some filtered photos. 😂📸

Practically, jokes on you. A millionaire would rather invest more in daily comfy shoes to walk more comfortably and stable. 👟💼

The complex mind introduced by the vices of society fights for who's on top. Those who focus on that ugly, shapeless competition forget their own paths and start competing for no reason other than childish plays like, "I've got Nikes, Apple, and Mercedes," while you live as a weak dog on ration food. 🥇🍏🚗🐕

And it’s what I call life: simple. You’re yourself making it hard by giving into envy and distraction of the world we have created around, the world of fake luxuries. Because no luxury clothes can make your body look ripped and good if you’re fat and ugly—ugly in the sense that you’re not in shape and you can’t run 2 km on command, a healthy person can. Sorry if it’s a bit of a rant, but it is what I say: the simple truth. 💪🧘‍♂️💫

Investing in daily activities is crucial. Promise yourself and your body a healthy, nutritious diet. 🥦 Give your mind some alone time with meditation. 🧘‍♂️

Stay distraction-free from that one thing in your hand that your mum says ruins your life: your mobile phone. 📱 Turn off that "device" for an hour or two and let it rest while you rest, distraction-free. 🌿 Actually give anything a thought – just think about it, completely alone and still. 

By that meditation – I call it "phone-free meditation" – you engage in a whole-day process. This is unlike the Western mantra of practicing it for 15 minutes a day. 🕒

To be fully meditated, you need to be more distraction-free and present in reality. The first barrier to that enlightenment is your phone, a device created by corporations to keep you tuned into the matrix. 🌐 Your whole perception of life depends on what is live on social media platforms, which manipulates you a lot subconsciously.

Some social media experts and fact-checking pages on Instagram claim they are authentic. 📖 But remember, it's essential to stay aware and discerning.

And you’re an owl who believes what they say without giving it any thought. 🦉 Moreover, you do not need that information, like what happened in Europe or Arabia—it's not any of your business. How funny videos make you laugh for a few minutes, then you're back to scrolling. As more worries wander through your mind, you already know what to watch to feel good. It's not the corn 🌽 dog show some secretly watch, but rather the sort of fake happiness you gain from watching car videos on repeat till 1 AM. The next day, it's some movie or series while you sit alone, apart from family and friends, while they are in the same boat. 🚤

As you tend to be all connected via social media, it is still no equivalent to face-to-face conversation with people you really love. ❤️ I would simply say that life is not about having more; having less is fine if it's of quality. OGs are not something to show off on the weekend; it is your daily hustle. 💪 Your daily hustle you can represent and proudly say you're proud of. So, simply, don’t think too much about it. 💭

Live simple 🌱. Make your daily routine simple 🕰️. Follow your own path 🛤️. Be an OG ✨. And, as I always say, always remember God in your heart ❤️ and don't forget your roots and culture 🌍. Your identity—your OG phenomenon—is what makes you different from the crowd. Stand tall and strong, be yourself 🌟.
