## There's so much out there, so much to do and so much to learn. Only to realise that you don't need much as your disease needs a specific medicine.



After been done with my 9 to 5 routine where I was working hours, commuting home and writing something in my free time. Sometimes personal, sometimes for somebody to see it and feel personal and helpful, so that is how I started writing. Writing with no goal and no objective other than self-help journaling and maybe showcasing.

To me, writing was all fun and joy initially as being part of my daily routine besides 9 to 5 because I was writing just with no purpose, however until my writings started getting noticed and people have taken to advice me with their financial perspective. To make some dough, some money out of my personal craft as what they said.

So I got all professional to put some graphics, why not to make a point, illustrate it further as I would imagine, So I started Substack with all fancy font and have a dedicated website for my craft as all my endless friends and visitors told me endless times to make a portfolio, make money out of my craft.

However, to only make things worse, I was constantly thinking new ideas, new things to write out so I can have more and more articles on the wall, So somehow I can milk a cash cow.

But boy, was I wrong with my craft to monetise only to realise that it is not me, that advises so far 100, has been kind and caring, but wrong. Wrong not for who cared to share, but for me who to act on. Because my writing was originally intended for the self help. For the purpose that someone is struggling similarly to how I have been.

To only realise so far after long gone silence, after long gone thought process, after long gone learning news things and reading new books.

To point of experience, as I would describe life feels purposeless and to all motivational social media posts, which says “to have a mission, to have a life goal” is absolute garbage, pure rubbish. It feels like masses agenda to get masses into rigid thinking processes where you’re thinking more and doing less.

As you watch one more reel or one more shorts about some millionaires making millions since young age, we feel useless, lazy and guilty for not working as they have made to own luxury cars and to travel the world.

To greater extent, It is to sell you courses and to confuse you as similarly I have outlined in [make your own path](https://open.substack.com/pub/harkseehra/p/make-your-own-path?r=28f97a&utm_campaign=post&utm_medium=web), only to realise that you are trapped, trapped inside that screen that all your focus is on watching these internet millionaires to motivate you for a minute or two.

So ending to this rant about horizontal screen destroying your focus to make a simple point - you feel purposeless, and adding to this negative ideology and perspective, I say life is purposeless. To live a life still - purposeless - to have small happiness through times, to live in a small house, to drive an old car, to wear regular old clothes is what a reality of time is and so should we be satisfied for what we have, to live a life, to let life flow without hoping to extract too much purpose out of life to actually do something.

However you can make better plans to make that life flow better like an idea of building better habits on daily basis to have a better routine, including habits like journaling, exercising, meditation and self reflection does not need fancy equipment but a commitment.

So to conclusion, to mark, I say live a life purposeless, build healthy habits, make short term plans with achievable goals. Don’t aim too high without an effective plan, love yourself <3.

Don’t tap into social media reality and social media induce reality because that is not real reality only to be derailed from your actual self and the dreams you have. As making money comes secondary after you make your career similar to any instant gratification pleasure comes after you take care of yourself physically, mentally and emotionally.
