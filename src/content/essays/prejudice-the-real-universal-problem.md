> #### The problem is our own idealistic, moralistic views that is again influenced by social standards and how we are programmed from our own childhoods.

The problem is a simple world called prejudice or in simple terms assumption. The prejudice is a real universal problem. Again the problem seeing problems in others, while focusing on our own problems. As we are seeing problems in others, we expect them to behave in certain way and force to be a certain way.

It reflects on even smaller things like the choice of clothing, food and culture. No one could be impartial and it is perfectly okay to be partial but having prejudice roots from our own insecurities we ignore and at same reflects our own shortcomings and defeats.

The things which are so elementary yet we ignore on day to day basis, the things which are to be avoided and ignored since day 1 now have become an addiction. As we tend to judge the world, we become unforgiving.
