There was me, then there was my mum, then father, and then a person who my mum and dad introduced: God. My mum and dad were so positive about God; they told me He had sent some of His avatars into the world because He’s the creator who lives up in the sky, meaning He lives in the universe. 🌟

And his avatars, who were gods themselves, wrote some scriptures that talked about life, philosophy, and many things about how life should be lived and proceeded. In these scriptures, they always talked about honesty, integrity, and love in life. The gods in the scriptures conveyed this in very positive ways, and as a child, I also became very devoted. 🙏✨

![unsplash](/images/essays-assets/unsplash%20(39).jpeg)

But as growing up when finally ***your eyes become your eyes*** and your thoughts become more independent and if you get good company you become good, unfortunately that's not what usually happens. How the people who followed religion was opposite to what god has said and even to more it was into much extremities that the child’s mind could not imagine.

So, growing up, I became agnostic and an atheist over time. But growing up, I realized and felt within myself that there is a god. While it is not the same or even close to what my parents, society, and people of religions have tried to teach me, my personal feeling is that God is an energy, a force, and a power that drives you to be your personal best. It helps you achieve a strong perspective where you see yourself as one and others as another. 🌟

> ## What Shapes Perception of God?

An individual’s thinking and thought process are on the same road. One may think that God is a person, a figure with a name and a voice. So, when you imagine God, you don’t see God but a good actor who has played God. Where does this come from? A good movie about God your parents might have shown you to be devout. The actor who played God or sounded like God may be great, so you tune in to a person who speaks about God to listen to the person but not to God. Often, the person who talks about God is presenting their view of God—all positive but very common. Nobody talks about how they felt God. As we all live on the same earth, and hopefully nobody sleeps daily on an airplane, it’s neutrally okay. 🌍✈️

But my perception is: as God being an energy, it is felt the same or differently by others—a form of pure energy when your mind connects with your body and your soul connects with your thoughts. Now you feel good about yourself as you’re on the path of improvement, a path of improvement where you are actually making efforts to make yourself happy, insightful, and natural. ✨

Leaving all the negative emotions behind and dealing with them, feeling God as an energy who is providing you with time and a journey to just experience what God has set as your ultimate destination of fulfillment. Here, you feel complete and yet grow every day, becoming bigger, always living one day longer than the previous day. Every day is designed differently, with both good and bad experiences to aid you in life. My philosophy is: if you take it as an experience, you will live; but if you take it as an illness, it will kill you. 🌟

> ## Ultimately, the experience of God streams within you!

Reminiscing about how I was a devotee when I was a child, I had the same positive feelings to grow in life. I felt God's presence during the hard times in my life. I learned that the more suffering and sacrifice you are willing to endure for yourself, the closer you are to God. 🙏

More ego, more negativity, more self-image protection, more old anger, more old life—you let it go, and the more you feel close to God. Then I feel it is the same; these many scriptures of many religions have been talking about the same thing. But as a human growing up, I have understood that it is a sacrifice in the world of evil habits, which makes the person a negative ‘me,’ where everything is ‘just me.’ The reason for such a person to exist is to be ‘selfish, angry, proclaimed.’ In a world where everyone is like that, nobody wants to deal with such a person. 🌟

But the feeling of God is you sacrificing yourself, the negative ‘me,’ and being the person God created you to be. This means making yourself and everyone else happy in the world, and coming up with ideas to improve the world above your greed. Do your part in life to improve your own and others' lives, because the feeling of God is to take God's thinking—how He thinks about everyone and then Himself. This thinking is how He's become a God, with the sacrifice of bad habits. Scriptures talk about how individuals must live with cleanliness, wake up early, how to eat, when to eat, etc. 🌟

The idea of God is taken by many cultures to preserve the culture. Many cultures used the idea of God to spread themselves in the form of religion. Many cultures have similar habits and similar foods, so the people of the culture have decided to feel comfortable outside their homes. They also come up with the idea to have more people think the same way as them, so they are the elitists in their own game. That is why many religions try to convert as many people to their religion, but only those who truly understand that the idea of God is a personal feeling realize that nobody has seen God and nobody would see. Thus, God could be different to different people. 🌍🙏

> ## GOD HINTS, YOU'RE THE FOLLOWER!!

My thinking is some people who have felt God have elaborated it with myth, some with scriptures, some with similar feelings, and much more. They added and updated the scripture, but each and every chapter always starts with your look at yourself, how to improve it, how to be the best version of yourself, and how to keep yourself happy. The idea of God is that nobody has seen it, but everyone has felt it. 🌟

My way of feeling it is, if you look closely, there is always something if you just exhale and drop your thinking for 5 minutes. While you are close to nature, it tells you a different story each day. The first essence is if you’re brave enough to listen to it, because usually the idea of self-improvement is hard; it takes a lot of self-criticism to get over the mind block and actually make some self-improvement. The second is if you feel everything is different from each moment, and to be actually capable of living each moment and articulating it. 🌿

The problem with modern-day philosophy is how they are always high on happiness, efficiency, and self-esteem, like how they are willing to eliminate negative thoughts. But the feeling of God tells me the importance of each feeling, whether it’s negative or positive. Everything, every person, every moment is significant, as important as it could be, and it allows my eyes to open further and have a wide perspective to see what’s going on above what’s being broadcasted. Understanding the importance of aligning my goals with my feelings is the gift God has given me. 🌟

So yeah, there is God. I feel it, not in the form of religion, but as a matter, an energy that knits everything together in a way that life just connects and disconnects to help you improve yourself. And if somebody dies early, for any reason, my thinking is that it's what God wants. Because you could've died worse from your worst habits, it's God having mercy on you to take that physical pain away and free the soul. May God transform me into that energy, the energy which performs a better part in this world and is remembered by everyone. 🙏
