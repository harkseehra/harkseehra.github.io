> # It’s more like a personal thing than a curated article.

So inspired by true events, I was talking with my homie just discussing life chilling, as soon as I asked him “how’s life treating you?”, his reply was “bad, I couldn’t tell you it’s that bad”, upon which I said “fair enough, why don’t you speak some truth?”, he said “the problems in life are increasing day by day”.

Upon which I think and I told him that life is like a war, a warrior while he goes to frontline has two choices - either to get shot easy and die easy, or to fight everyday for their family, their children and their nation.

The warrior who gets shot easy has no respect, is just in dead list and doesn’t achieve any military award and the warrior in that state isn’t even considered warrior.

But one who goes to frontline everyday saving himself and fighting is fighting for their family and children to be on frontline more if army pays for your family food and expenses and if the warrior dies - they receive a special award, a statue and probably a free ration to their whole family, free education to their children and what not special warrior could have provided.

That’s what I call warrior mentality, I personally think everyone should be a warrior, a fighter and one who puts himself in front and fight for their existence and make their existence known by their hard work, ambition and dedication.

The real reason is not because you have to prove someone, but you have to prove yourself, which makes you confident. And confidence brings a chance for you to shine, be successful.

But a successful warrior is still a warrior and life is a long war, therefore rest more than required is not ever allowed, even the people who have won wars are always preparing for the new war.

> #### There is no any time to be ever comfortable. Comfort is killing, pain gives you living.

Like for example a well settled musician understands the importance of hard work, so they never would settle down to only sing till they are in their 40s, they would work until they die.

> **And to me, I have that sort of mindset that working until they die is a beautiful thing. Depends upon if they love what they are doing.**


The warriors fight for themselves, there is always a reason, always a dream, an inspiration to start from self like what yourself means to exist.

> #### It happens with what is your childhood dream, a warrior’s dream is to go to war and fight for the right.


If the warrior isn’t fighting for what he consider his big assets, he wouldn’t fight and give up easily. The big assets which justify his existence are their family and heritage.

But there is a self factor, a brave warrior would fight to display his bravery, a dangerous warrior would kill millions of opposition to display he’s a dangerous. A tough warrior would get wounds on his body, but still fights and never gives up.

There is self, but a self motivated by your childhood dreams. Not the self motivated by worldly greed and to give in worldly greed and luxuries, you also adopt to what the world expect from you.

The warriors focus is always on target, not by some self indulged vices which distracts him from his purpose. The purpose of warrior is to win the war.

> #### To win the war, the size of army doesn’t matter but the strategy of how you use army.

To win the war, the warrior must have a goal, a focus, a craze in their thinking how to win the war, nothing else if warrior sleeps, eats or drink matters.

> #### **There’s a focus driving human species, somehow elites are trying to stop their focus on themselves to focus on what they are selling.**

The warrior thinks of a plan, a strategy and mainly an action and direction to win the war.
Even the fallen soldiers are respected, at least they fought.

> ### Moreover why who has given up dies faster and not remembered?

People say there is a mythology and then there is truth. The mythology couldn’t happen realistically, so please tell the truth. But I think everything is a truth, for my experience people who give up die faster and also not remembered by their loved ones because what they have done except giving up?

So many people are giving up, losing focus and they tend to give up on their mental ability, their fitness, their looks and their personality. While I personally think everything matters of what I mentioned to live a purposeful life.

So what they do? They try to compensate their Ls, their giving up traits by their overexposed positive proud traits. Like a nerd would overcompensate on their mental ability to hide their ugly unfit body.

The people who give up their mental ability try to look tough by working in jobs which leach their blood. It’s so great I work low paying hard labor job that even my toenails are falling, but I still love it.

The problem is not sorting out their problems, and simply refusing to fight their wars, more like tackling their problem which gifts one depression, anxiety and more of what I call a “mask”.

> #### As you get older, the mask just now looks more wrinkly, suddenly old people start maturing, maybe we couldn’t hide it - there are wrinkles.

The mind could lie, the body couldn’t. Everything you give up on reflects on their body. The people who seek comfort and gives up easily die faster because you are not struggling.

Then there is a problem. Because even for a regional artist, their dedication and struggle tends towards becoming a national artist, then there is national artist working 16 hours on movie set to be an international artist.

> #### There is always a struggle, but there are two choices - you can either struggle or choose comfort.

The people who have chosen comfort are comfortable to live in cheap apartments, eat bad food, compromise on their health, have addictions to even set them more comfort.

> #### Now not they would die faster with such lifestyle but their loved ones would rather be happy on them dying because they were always a liability.
