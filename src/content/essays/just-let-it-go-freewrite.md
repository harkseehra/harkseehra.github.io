> ## let it go because it is not yours and it is you.

As life starts, we come as babies into the world. 🌎 Life seems new, life seems like nothing, life seems entertaining, life seems indie. Life seems full of possibilities, both for a baby and their parents.

Parents think the best for their children: how they are going to provide them with the best education and set up their future plans. For the baby, the world is new. 🍼 The baby picks up the world as dogs have noses and cats have eyes, with all the expressions. Full of positive energy, the new human being brings brightness into the room. ☀️

But when the baby cries, the room turns into shackles. 🥺 When it smiles and giggles, parents hope to have more children because of the joy and energy a new human brings into the world. 😄

As life happens, we as children see many things, many ups and downs. No matter how good a society you live in, nothing can hide the worries and sorrows of the world. 😔

For parents, the best practice is to hide these worries in plain sight. They think, "Why pass worry to growing children? Let them be children for a long time." 🧸

To parents, children are always children. 👶 They have in their conscious mind seen the child grow from age 0 to 18 or keep track of the time in between, as life happens.

But as we grow from children to adults, we now understand our parents. We understand why they said certain things and why they stopped us from doing this or that. 🚫 Because, in the end, there is pain after indulging in any forbidden fruit. 🍎 There is pain after any unfair fight you have won. 🥊 There is pain anytime you think of yourself before your loved ones. 💔

Anytime there is greed, lust, or anything that doesn’t align with God’s teachings or cultural values, it leads to suffering. 🙏🌍 These lessons are first taught by our parents because they have experienced the same things. They don’t want us to experience the pain, the bitter fruits of our actions. 🍂

> ## Yet, as we grow, we start taking what we want. 🤲 What do we make of what we take? 🌱

The world’s anxiety and depression, fueled by the wrong decisions, could have been prevented. 🌎😔 

If we just listened to what our parents are saying, what our culture says, and what our religion advises, we could avoid many pitfalls. 👨‍👩‍👦📜🙏 

Understanding who we are and acknowledging our roots can lead to eternal peace. 🌳🕊️ 

As the third generation, grandparents often barge in with good words of religion. We foolishly label them as outdated or religiously brainwashed, as the society in the West describes them this way merely to justify their vices. 🚪👵🗣️ 

This allows them to commit silent crimes against the rise of humanity and our human race. 😞🔇

In return, they only promote drugs, illicit sex, and other places to get relaxed by non-spiritual means. 💊❌🛤️ 

For any sane human being, this adds up to guilt every time you turn dark. And since it is not the first time you ignore your parents' advice, the guilt becomes overwhelming. 😔🔁 

It becomes so much that you just want to go back and restart. ⏪🔄

To make a new identity, where you are all fair and square and as people progress through age they make a new identity, they change, they change for better but only better is soon when you understand these few basic things to understand who you are and why you are.

Why you are is your parents decided to have you, and who you are is your own destiny. It is a half-and-half decision, so stakes are half-and-half because they put trust in you to come into this amazing world. As we all grow older, and day by day as our grandparents say, life is never about money, fame, or anything but it is more about doing whatever you want as your child version of you would do. Do the right thing. As children, you may have seen your parents gone and done wrong, same with society, same with religion, and same with culture. 🌟🌍👶🏻💪🏽

To the point, there are so many things to fix, so many problems to face. Already, the present would be so hard, or if you do not accept it, like some people who hold their noses higher than their problems, they never accept it. As simple as it sounds, the natural occurrence of time happens. Human beings are bound to emotions, but it is the brain that converts our anger into envy and changes our sadness into depression. 🌟🧠💔

The only solution is to let it go if it is not yours. As a human being, you are bound to first take care of yourself physically and mentally to fix other things. In simple words, you’ve got to fix yourself by letting go of what is not yours. If it is yours, and at some point in time, you did not play fair and may have won, but guilt is bugging you about your dishonesty and selfish thinking, then improve yourself. Nourish your mind by doing the opposite—like a little philanthropy doesn’t hurt as much as not visiting God’s house from time to time 🙏.

Let it go—your pain, depression, and anxiety. If it is not yours, if it is not what you think is wrong, let it go. Some betrayals have not been caused by you, but you carry the pain. Let it go. Because those who commit wrong deeds, I promise Karma deals with them later. Their guilt starts from day one, and as with every psychological effect, there is a physiological outcome. Their health starts dropping, and it is purely visible. So, if somebody has betrayed you, next time you meet them, just look them in the eyes. You will be free. ✨💖
