Anxiety is often referred in medical terms as the disease that makes a person paranoid over small things like their decisions, people in general, and society overall — the societal fear. It’s a disease of a person’s inability to stay in the real world because, in their minds, they’re somewhere else, the world they have created with the promise that it’s going to protect you. But now, it feels like a prison.

The anxiety, to my definition, is an alarm system developed by the human brain to protect itself. At first, it shows itself as the finest and fittest. Then, over the constant rejection after all your weak efforts, you start to grow into a person who is now in command of the world, in the sense of what the world wants.

In actuality, though, no one in the world wants that. That is the state of mind at the start of it, as if the mind has taken the decision to start something. The body will adopt it, and now you’re jacked up mentally and physically to be a person you think the world wants — to fit in, to look common, which is again a disease to the creative mind gifted by God.

The start of human beings was successful because, to be human, the literal definition is to adopt. But adoption, in this scientific context, is to adapt to the situation. Yet, these anxious people adopt to society and strive to be classified in one group of society. The boundaries set them aside again. Anxious people haven’t been meant to be what the world wants, as the world is much kinder if you’re kinder. But as they now become what the world wants, the world takes advantage of them.

The anxiety to a child is simple — little things turning into big issues. You’re not good at sports; you’re not meant for sports. You’re not a good singer; you’re not for music. To a child’s mind, there are many supporters, but just because some bully or jerk told us something hurtful, we’re still anxious about what started in childhood.

The perception of an anxious person is with the misconception that they’re not capable, so they must up their downplay with some other level of magic to earn anyone’s respect. Because respect is a thing that is still not sold anywhere but to one’s feeling.
