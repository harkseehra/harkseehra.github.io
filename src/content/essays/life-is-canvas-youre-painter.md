> ## as simple and subtle it sounds, it means exactly what you think, but question wanders how to be good painter?

Life is a canvas and you’re THE painter of your life. 🎨 You can make it all bright blue, yellow, and green with perfect roses and petals by blending your personality, your motivation, and, most importantly, your intentions. 🌹✨ With all that, the consequences can be favorable, bringing peace and calm. 🌟🕊️

But if you don’t get it right and start living to paint too much, rather than painting it slowly, gently, and peacefully, the objective of life changes for many people as they navigate society. They begin to believe that the must-have things in life are money, power, and special abilities—superpowers some geeks seek from their brains and muscle builders from their muscles—to be extraordinary. 💪💡

While all this sounds shiny and glittery, you may want to use these glittery, shiny materials in your life because you live to impress others. This makes only one point: you are focused on the paintings of others when you, yourself, can be an artist and let people admire you for what you do. 🎨✨

The people who understand that are what they say in world terms are successful. They used less paint, kept their heads straight to their goals and childhood dreams, and focused on what they actually want. This is because they are connected to their core and roots of who they truly are. Even if they have changed from what their friends remember from their childhood, who they initially were, were they so jolly back then? 

But now, you come with an inflated ego of worldly achievements which are not even Honorable. At least when you sleep with 100 people or do drugs or other random crazy stuff, which to these teens and even today's adulterous couples behind each other's backs, what thrills does that forbidden fruit give that all other morals and motives go blind? 🌍🎨💭🌟👶🌈

Where could you have gotten it right, and where have things gone wrong? 🤔 These are the questions people ask themselves, including you and me. I would say that when your intentions are not right, neither are your morals when trying to achieve anything. Greed comes to mind, and honesty goes out the window because who wouldn’t want to watch a 75-inch LED TV 📺 instead of going on a greasy, dirty nature trail? Only those who have extra fat on their body, who also greed for food. As soon as they start feeling a bit empty, they eat to relieve stress and emptiness. 

When one feels empty and stressed, the body and mind work together, and you torture them by eliminating all toxins and foregoing all the bad fat. This process takes 3-5 months of a daily disciplined, curated diet and exercise. The same goes for the mind and life: to make changes, you’ve got to change your colours 🌈 and not just hope for more; instead, work for more and deserve more. Like they say, work hard and play harder. 💪

Life's only motivation is upward, where you can see yourself at the age of 80 or older, alive, well, and healthy, without the dopamine rushes from wrong activities like drug and alcohol abuse. 🚫🍷 Your future is bright if you choose the right path! 🌟

Many people use these dopamine rushes. Some call it addiction; I would say it's easy gratification for anything that provides relief. I call it relief from any emotional distress because life happens—it is not easy, rather hay-wired. 😖 You get emotional nonsense from everyone revolving around you, and many people admit it by complaining. Some call them saints for forgiving and having goodwill, but only if you knew you could make something better from the start by dealing with it with good intentions. 💭

As human beings with hearts and eyes, we always deal best with those who have good intentions and who we know are honest. 🤝 You wouldn’t even think of stealing from them. Your relationships flourish as you’re open and have no closet full of skeletons, with nothing to hide. You do not lust for everyone; only one would be acceptable if you believe in love and the ideology that life can be a mystery. 🌫️ It can be like windy gray days or scorching sun on summer days, or it could be much more if you make much more of it. The only way to avoid what happens and what has happened is to go back and start from the beginning. 🌅

Start using more bright canvas colours like white to display purity, sky blue to show openness like a blue sky, and yellow to show you’re happy and all dandy. 🎨✨ Make your life’s canvas be considered as art rather than painting it all gray or mismatched for people to figure out, and for you to hide behind fake sympathy. If somebody would help to heal at the end stage of whatever vice you have, like trusting your friends and parents, stop using them as a helpline. Instead, focus on what makes them happy. You want them happy, not worried and sick because of what you have become. You could say not everyone is perfect and straight-lined, and life is like a puzzle; 🧩 I say in putting puzzle pieces together, you need a will before you use your brain. Because if your will doesn’t work, the puzzle doesn’t solve. Life is literally like that. 🌟

You must deal with it from head to toe; otherwise, the problem doesn’t get solved. Intoxication can provide relief and mask the problem for a while, but overindulgence has long-term effects, making you more reliant on it to hide behind a so-called phase of life. Straighten up by discarding all those mixed colors that conceal you and your emotions, even from yourself, before you think about hiding them from others. If you hide, you become shady, and nobody likes shady people. You can't hide using intoxication. By intoxication, I mean any activity that gives you a rush and helps you sleep, because if you stop, you will experience withdrawal symptoms, even from small activities that aren't typically classified as addictions. 🌈✨

---

To me, an addiction is anything basic. People use their mobile phones 📱 to deal with anxiety and the anxiety of not showing expressions of depression going on in their minds. People overeat 🍔 when they are stressed and feeling empty, low on motivation, and as lazy as they have gotten. Their will to make improvements vanishes in procrastination, turning to other resources which science says are technically addictions, like drugs 💊 and porn 📺. Anything that corrupts your mind and decreases your moral values becomes an addiction. 

Whilst you hide, you get very good at hiding and masking. What was once illegal starts to feel legal, and life feels thrilling. But just like thriller movies end with someone getting killed, you are seasoned. A good Samaritan would still die inside your mind daily, leading you back to hiding until it becomes chaotic and real sad. You might think you’re the saddest, and you could be, but as you tread ahead, you see there are a lot more and worse. The only way out from the depths of hell is to make a turn back 🌟.

Instead of paving a road to hell and drowning in sorrow, you could use that anger and frustration to deal with your problems directly, without any addictions. 💪 By doing so, you could stop and prepare yourself for a better life. History teaches us that it's not about guilt; it's about adapting to who we are as humans. 🌍 You can only adapt if you accept it first, like Germany did. As a basic rule of life and economics, it's better to have more assets than liabilities. 📈 You can create real-life assets by getting your head right in the world, speaking true words, being honest, and staying close to God and your roots. Following the doctrine of culture and religion (VERY IMPORTANT) are your real assets to become who you are. 🙏✨

And if people know who you are and what your brain is like—how honest and thorough you are in explaining yourself, how right and great you are—you can only display pride when you’re with good people. If some people deal wrong, then it is a misconception that good people will lose anything: money, reputation, or life. The people who have dealt wrongly only get guilt and humiliation from their actions. So, the better you are, the less it matters. Again, you wouldn’t use cheap color markers on your canvas of painting; you would use high pastel colours abstracted from natural flowers and trees. 🌸🌿
