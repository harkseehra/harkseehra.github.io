> ## Are you more left person or right? Do you like drinking at 3AM or you wake up at 6 daily? Your lefts and rights describe you of who you are.

Same as before, I said there should be equilibrium ⚖️— balance between two sides, left and right—whether in politics or geometry. It says the left-hand side is equal to the right-hand side. So, how do you make rational decisions while still leaning toward the "demonic" left? 🤔

For example, a child’s mind in buying a new car wants a muscle car—red, shiny, with yellow fancy graphics and big wheels. But you have the budget for a Camry mid-base model. So, what would you do? Would you increase the budget, making your financial life difficult? This means you will have to work more to afford the muscle car, as your left brain says—the child’s mind. 🚗💭💰

Well, some folks tend to listen to their left brain more, while some want stability and right-mindedness. They are taking their decisions well, rationally, for a more comfortable longevity and future 🌟. But the left brain says, "Why dull and gray? Why be boring? Why not shine more as we can grind more?" 🤔 The outcomes of both brains, thought of optimistically, only the future can tell. 🌈🔮

Well, humans are able to predict the future by carefully making decisions. 🤔 It’s in the nature of some right-minded people to make future plans without indulging in vices, falling into pitfalls, or facing problems. 🚫

But life is no science. It is not math either, where you add 2 and 2, and you get 4. 🔢 Life is more unpredictable and complex than that. 🌟

Instead, life is drama, emotions, love, betrayal, and what not on Netflix 🎭. The art of canvas the left brain would describe and see as to be happy: to not have suffering, to not have problems in life. It should be sunny outside, and while I come home at 11 PM, my mum does not fight with me. Or in my case – yes, mum, please tell me some life lesson so I can remember it 5 years later 📝. The left wants art, fun, and love to live because these are life priorities. As Italians say, so do Italians more lean to the left? 🇮🇹💖
