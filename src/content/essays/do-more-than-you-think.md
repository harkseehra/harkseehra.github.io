> ## That's no motivation, that's reality, if you want something in life - get it by doing more than you think and to go above the stairs in upward zigzag pattern.

Do more than you think, listen more than you speak, to eat only what you deserve rightfully and truthfully, and to produce more than you take advice for.

I would say stop advising and taking advice. Instead, trust yourself to make your decisions using your own knowledge. That trust can only be developed if you're proud of yourself for some real OG achievement driven by your passion to achieve more.🏆 More medals and trophies at your disposal lead to more pride—the real pride in achieving that dreamy goal. By grinding yourself, you promise to work harder and give 100% of yourself for only one person to care about, and that is yourself.💪✨

The path to your goal—a childhood dream to drive all real American muscle cars in blue jeans and a black leather jacket—is only possible and sustainable if you follow your passion. But if your passion is not enough to fill your 16 hours of the day, you may take your goal as a hobby. I call it a ‘life juggle’. In other words, life is a jungle, which means it’s wild, unknown, and uncertain, while you are protecting yourself and your loved ones to ensure your basic needs and security—shelter—are fulfilled. 🚗👖🧥🌟

It is only possible to live in the jungle of life if you are WILD yourself 🐅. Don't be like sheep in a herd, with goat-headed shepherds controlling you 🐑👨‍🌾, while you think they are your friends. You are what you are—a sheep 🐑. And sheep are used for their wool and meat 🐏🥩. Take the metaphor to mean just that: sheep being humans and the shepherds are people in power 👥💼.

To put it more rhetorically: you need to be alert while hunting for prey 🦌 and achieving real-life goals. For lions, this means mating and producing powerful offspring 🦁. To do so, they need to fight and defeat other lions to become the top lion in the lion kingdom 👑.

While in humans, we cannot go brawl in the streets to be TOP G—especially as we follow law and know the cops—we tend to compete in worldly achievements. 🏆 Both are true in human and animal nature: those who focus on themselves, grind daily, work on their muscles, and hustle, WIN the jungle, the world. 💪🌍

While who focus on others and what others are doing, forget their own goals and hustle, as they get weak and face death, while it is only true for humans - the killing part is on psychological level when that lost battles convert into depression later anxiety.

But you can save yourself by only doing little extra than you currently do as routine. To atleast do that little extra and increasing little extra to little extra you have done the yesterday, days ago to only make the chart of that little extra to be an upward slope until you are 60, 70 or 80s, until your limps lose sensitivity and your brain becomes slow.

That little extra is what that creates a strong mind, strong body and main thing that you need in your life which is discipline by only making a plan, being productive, writing journals and creating checklists just to keep yourself on track and to do only little extra than your job for your passion and if you are already doing your passion then self improvement.

To invest in things in life which only enhance your life quality on daily basis like basic good habits building like making and following a schedule, so you value time to remember why at first place for the goal you are living and making your life for.

> ## It is time to finally get moving and working, No more lazy - unproductive and unmeaningful days, value time and yourself.
