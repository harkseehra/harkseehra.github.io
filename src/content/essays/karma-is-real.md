Karma is something out of imaginary as I had imagined. I never believed in karma and to extent I didn’t understand the karma, to me karma was just a revenge, a revenge taken by god of your wrongdoings against some people in the world, so people say don’t be bad, don’t do bad because karma will get you.

Moreover for the religions, who somewhere understand karma more as in their perception- they defined it in two categories- simply put bad and good karma. Means karma is a universal thing that if you do a good thing, you get a great return and vice versa with bad karma, you will get axes 🪓 if you pull a knife on innocent, so the greater effect of the karma.

And they say karma is something that it happens after time, it has its time to get you, punish you or reward you.

Well any of these definitions could be correct and useful in conveying the message that don’t do bad deeds because law of motion defines equal reaction to the action- as simply karma is defined.

More realistically to what I have experienced, sharing my experience, karma is a realistic thing, it’s like a rubber band with elastic force, you may pull in and aim for the target- whatever your intentions are- the moment you pull it and aim, you don’t realise rubber band instantly hits you back with same force. So karma hits you instantly, it starts but it’s just now some guilt, you may think you can deal with this new wound as it’s just small cut, you can put bandage on and forget it.

---

![unsplash](/images/essays-assets/unsplash%20(15).jpeg)

But Karma’s wound is real, with all medicines, bandages and tapes, you cover it on the nights, you give yourself hope and motivation that next day this wound is going to fill up and recover but sad thing about karma is it never fills up until it takes 100 times of pain from you.

And the funny part, or should I say realisation as reality now feels funny and now you understand that you have been being dumb and treating a wound with wrong medicine, putting bandages over the deep wound, it’s not a paper-cut, but it’s more deeper than a bullet.

You start understanding reality as how and why Karma is and that to point you understand karma that all the wrongdoings, all the ills are making you sick but only to point of breakdown, we understand the occasional pain in our kidney is not a stone or gastrointestinal issues, but a cancer which could’ve been avoided if we could’ve understood, understood our body and our mind.

Same with Karma, if we could’ve understood and stopped our wrongdoings, our sins and our wrong intentions, whatever sick and twisted for the instant pleasure, instant fame and money- something we do not deserve but somehow bending the frame of time by our conman-ship, we achieved it, maybe by mockery, deceit and lies.

Karma would’ve been kinder to us, karma could’ve made it clear sooner but it’s nature is hideous as our intentions are, it only reveals itself when it breaks down everything, by everything I mean everything.

It breaks you mentally, physically and emotionally. It exhausts you, it puts you down to the ground and until your nose is completely submitted to the ground, it doesn’t ever leave because nose is a part which we humans can control which is not meant to be control, it reflects the truth and true emotions, but as you start using bending and twisting it for your evil mind, karma makes sure it breaks down as your eyes go watery and your head goes heavy like rock like the guilt and pain has become so much.

So that’s what Karma is, it bends you, it twists you, it makes you look crooked, it grants you slow painful death.

But you could avoid it, you can repent it, you can only weight it by doing good karma against your balance of negative karma.

### Karma is real, karma is brutal, karma works mysteriously and it’s instant.
