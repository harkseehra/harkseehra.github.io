> ## Discover who you are and what to prioritize in life, because focus is at the core of everything, like the center of every circle and rectangle.

Every power you have in you is situated not just where you’re training. You might be training your physical health by building some muscle and doing some cardiovascular exercises, and your mental health by reading books, watching good movies, and feeding your mind with positive thoughts. Watching positive series like Breaking Bad and El Chapo can also contribute. Whatever you are doing, however, is useless unless you maintain it. To maintain it, you need focus. In physical terms, focus might mean concentrating on one single task at a time. For example, when you’re focusing on reading a book, but your mind can only maintain focus for more than 2 minutes. Suddenly, after 2 minutes pass, your mind starts wandering because you’re willing to sacrifice focus for something more fun. 

However, to delve deeper into physical focus, your eyes start wandering, and the words become blurry. You might find yourself just reading the words while thinking of something else. Then, you read a bit between the lines, and the interest is gone. Too sad. 📚💪🧠

It’s more about maintaining your eyes' focus on the words than anything else. 👀✨ When focus disrupts, the flow doesn’t continue, and things just seem to lose interest. 🚫📖

It's a serious problem, not only in reading but in living life on a day-to-day basis. 🚶‍♂️🗓️ I feel individuals are so out of their rhythms; their words and how they interact and express tell two different stories because of a loss of focus. 🗣️🔄

This loss of focus in the center is similar to what is mentioned in both yogic and modern scientific texts about the human’s 6th sense, which relies on the third eye, located 3-4 cm above the eyes' length in the center. 🧘‍♂️🔬

That is where they say it is physically present, the human’s third eye, because it has all the tissues the human eyes have, but it is buried inside the skull. 👁️🧠

The god’s identity could be anything, and it's the same with training your brain to be anything, to do anything—to unlock yourself from you and be the ultimate you. It’s an upward journey; you become the god yourself. But do not hold yourself as God—instead, read what God has said. Many cultures made that point and, as Jesse Pinkman would say, “Yo, you’re not listening to your religion and culture, bitch.” 🤯🧘‍♂️📚

I say, do not believe in God and become blind followers. Instead, believe and apply the things God has said in the texts to unlock your true potential. 🌟 Your parents brought you into this world, giving themselves to create the beautiful hybrid version of them in you, possessing both of their features. 👨‍👩‍👧 

Every human being has two sides, masculine and feminine. It is about what we are building inside. We are only perfect humans if we learn to develop these sides, achieving the perfect combination of blue and pink as God intended. 💙💗 God has provided us with enough context, yet we remain ignorant, thinking He would just pour honey on us if we go to church or temple daily. 🕍⛪

Scientifically, XX and XY chromosomes determine sex. The XX identifies as female, meaning it is just more feminine. True femininity, embodied by love, care, and compassion, is often discarded. Nowadays, women sometimes embrace the XY characteristics, developing their 'blue' side to mimic men, wrongly identifying their potential. 💪👩‍🎤

This happens because we do not recognize our true selves. Let's strive to understand and build both sides within us, as intended. 🌈

We do not recognize our centre. With the centre comes the core. You can have a centre only when you have a core. 🧘‍♀️ The core, as described by philosophical texts and religion, is about embodying real values like morality, honesty, and being free from greed and lust. 🌱

Religion interprets this core as a doctrine of how humans should behave—not because of strict rules, but to enable humans to reach their true potential. Parents create their children to possess their best qualities, not their worst. 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Some individuals deviate from this path, either leaning too much to the right—conservative, holding too tightly to their beliefs—or being overly open-minded, resisting any form of capture by strict ideologies. This often results in envy, anger, and hatred towards the right. 😡

The problem lies in a lack of focus. Life is not about adhering to a linear mantra; rather, it is about maintaining one’s centre and focus. Many people lose their centre and thus lose their focus. 🎯

People often believe they are correct and righteous without further education. By their mid-20s, some feel they've had enough education, thinking that schools and colleges have overburdened their minds with unnecessary standards. 🎓 Courses are designed in a way that often confuses students, pushing them to pursue bachelor's degrees and then master's degrees, believing it takes a master to be a master. Yet, after completing a master's, many are more confused about their life direction than before.

Losing focus during childhood leads to unfulfilled dreams and unclear goals as adults. This results in individuals not recognizing who they truly are and what they genuinely want to do in life. 🌠

These are serious, life-longing questions. As the situation turns around, you realize that to adapt and do something new, life is not boring. Having change is not boring. The right-minded people understand it is super wasteful to enjoy moments watching Netflix and eating popcorn 🥤🍿 rather than actually doing some development.

In contrast to old-time stories where people worked hard for years and partied after making it 5, perhaps 10 years, nowadays, you party every weekend 🎉. It seems like you’re partying way more than you are working and achieving something. Instagram shows that millionaires are chilling, smoking weed, drinking booze, and going around with girls 🌿🍻. 

Funny enough, people adapt these as their life goals. They struggle financially yet drink booze every Friday. Why not start a business? Because as business says in a business tone, “business builds you wealth.” The real life is not about having money. The real reason some people are super rich and enjoying life now is because they grinded themselves. Obviously, not just for their business, but by doing what they love and developing something spectacular ✨.

To build something spectacular, you need to be spectacular yourself. It is about *you*. It is not about them or anyone else. Life is about *you*. Because life is *you*. Live your goddamn life and keep yourself happy.

However, as the doctrine of how humans should live reveals, to find oneself in the true sense of who they are and why they are created, it is not money, nor fame. It is what you actually want in life. What are your dreams? As you grow older, your brain rarely dreams anything of yourself. Life becomes all about the world and what happens in it 🌏.

Now, your aspirations are money, booze, and a partner—perhaps some family. While it is true that you owe every ounce of love and respect that other people give you back to them, tell them the same thing. It is about focus. It is about losing yourself for the new ‘you’ in the next day to find who you are 🔍.
