> ### Reading and Writing is more than you think, it's a human psychology

> #### I think I have one of biggest problems in human history which is read and write because same one the whole human generation have got, except few exceptional.

It is like when the cycle of life starts, the birth of any human, they are full of energy and their light brightens the room, they are unconscious of their blissful state, they are actively producing and writing something in people’s memories.

They are pure reflection of god, because the offspring of any living being on earth are very cute and innocent. The offspring is so tiny and precious, and has a large aura of what I call **‘god potential’.**

![unsplash](/images/essays-assets/unsplash%20(2).jpeg)

To just see a baby, toddler and child being themselves and enjoying life gives the child’s parent a joy, state of bliss which changes their mind to be more happy and connecting. The children also inspires lot of people with lost hopes, the children are again being themselves unconscious of what adults call **“harsh reality”.**

But as the toddler starts to turn into a kid, there is survival training, it is time to learn, it is time to read.

> Reading is although not a bad thing, but it is bad if you are reading bad things.

So parents enroll the kid into an elementary, where a kid is still providing the positive hope of potential is forced to read. Forced to learn society norms which are just bad.

The society never wants you to be successful, they want you to be like them, unsuccessful, driven by addictions and meaningless.

Therefore as the reading starts, the kid is now forced to become an adult, read more and always be reading since they care so much about you. The real reason is they are projecting their ideas into you and you are just reading and listening.

> #### There is lots of advice and I think taking and giving advice are both bad.

The society, school and family have their own expectations from you. The internet wants you to be entrepreneur and make a kicking. *(making living is for living, kicking is where you are extremely successful having huge yachts and luxury cars, a huge mansion with fire-pit is must).*

Where your family has society driven approach to your well being and follow a certain career path which makes you financially settled. But all in all, the person who actively engages in giving and taking advice is delusional and has no mind of their own.

They are actively reading what others are writing based on what they have read. The very good example is there are two groups in school. One is hood guys drinking and having parties, other one is a study group. Study group guys are losers, they are actually studying and living towards their purpose.

> That’s why I am always reader positive but depends on a reader where they hang out.

More and more, many people have lost their purpose as they have forgotten to write. Instead they have given into the narrative to realise later in life that self has positioned itself across what others expect of them which hardly matters.

What matters is if you have a purpose in life you live for. It’s close to many people giving up of fear what the society would say, or if their plan would ever work?

**“win the mind, win the world”.** The 90% of problem is in your mind, where the actual 10% is only you have to solve by an action. The problems are in their mind, their habits and their unhealthy lifestyle.

If any monkey business could bring quick cash to sustain lifestyle to only drink more. Drinking and morals never go together.

The survival the narrative of society is said super tough. But in history, since when there was survival easy? Only who think and act differently find their own way by writing and live like OGs.

People who follow sheep are herd. People who write, express and think different are **GOATS.**

This is universe’s providing one chance, one opportunity and one life to make it into the legacy. Being creator of your own reality.

By solving the fundamental problem of the century, where people have forgotten to write and express, where people have forgotten to speak and get themselves represented.

> #### My way is read less write more, you become reader when you read, you become LEGEND when you write.
