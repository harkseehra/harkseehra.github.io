The Hebrews (**بني اسرائيل**) were simple people being traders. They used to trade essentials, they believed in the law of possibility that if you can work hard - the reward shall be there.

Unfortunately After the death of prophet Joseph (**يوسف**), Pharoah at that time - the king of Egypt - looked at their hard work and skills - decided to imprison them and force them into harsh labour. These Hebrew traders were not only skilled and hard working. But one thing that was hereditary was the ability to adapt, to mold their clay as the situation arises.

The Pharoah decided to imprison these special sort of people who can work hard at median wages and if somebody would disobey - they would be punished harshly and sometimes even gets killed.

Therefore the granted and already blessed superior people of Egypt - Egyptians deemed themselves to be superior in their mold. The ego and cunning of Pharoah was motivated by fake sense of superiority and power. 

The generations of Hebrews went by approximately 150-200 years of imprisonment, After continuing tradition - New Pharoah of time was sitting with Astrologers.

The astrologers trembling and silent - afraid deciding if they should give Pharoah, the bad news, that reigning Pharoah shall be killed by the more intelligent, more powerful and one who will bring the correct order for Hebrews.

The reason was the harshness on believing Hebrews with their clothes torn and bread burned, still were thankful to God. On the contrary - the people of Pharoah, Egyptians were bred to be idol worshipers which led to their phenomenon of inferiority and superiority based on how one looks, what are good and bad morals of idols - stones - **بُت** - they were worshipers of.

---

The gods of paganism were as complex as the black magic. With the help of magic, people of Pharoah started eating of gluttony and became busy in shape sculpting. 

Because "One which looks is one which sells", people go over aesthetics over functionality. These were Egyptians beliefs at that time therefore they were deceitful and treating Hebrews in worst manner possible as they deemed Hebrews non superior.

Upon hearing the words of Astrologers, Pharoah was in dread, afraid for his life. He started massacre of all new born boys at that time when Moses was predicted to be born. But somehow God as commanded and written destiny Moses was discovered by Pharoah's sister. The prayers of Hebrews and the promise of "fulfilling nation" hold true.

Unknowingly, Pharoah did not know the one who Pharoah was raising is his own enemy, the one who brings justice.

While Moses was growing up in royal household not knowing his mother and people were only guided by God for righteousness. While  Idol worshipers were sharpening their stone like externally polished but rotten inside were Egyptians under those Egoistic commander.

Instead of worshiping God, Pharoah was talking with various demigods to make him God to reign over people. He deemed himself superior and judging people under hierarchy based on various factors of his own Godly parameters. Therefore God, the almighty has to show his might, make it manifest that he remains as the only one. So Prophet Moses (**موسی**) was God's amazing written plan to stop Pharoah's attributes amongst Egyptians - the idol makers.

---

When the corruptness reaches new heights, God has to bring an order therefore the prayers of believers and mercy can still save and purify rotten people. Prophet Moses reminisced his origin by Godly grace one evening, when he saw harsh behaviour of Egyptian guard on a Hebrew slave and killed the guard.

As the warrior of reality, he had to hit the rotten one. As Pharoah heard the news - he had a tooth ache - he immediately ordered his guards to imprison Moses. But the consciousness of  Moses ran to **مدین** - Madyan to live in the tribe. 

The Egyptian perceptively - the man of royal blood had to accept his reality of who he really was.

Moses became believer out of tribulations and hardness he had to endure. Fighting and trembling he became the protector of women as the trial of **صفورا** - Zipphora, the cunning of Zipphora saw the believer inside Moses clay.  Truly "*Corrupt women are for corrupt men, and corrupt men are for corrupt women; and good women are for good men, and good men are for good women"*  holds. 

Zipphora convinced his father to settle Moses in tribe - only later to be married to Zipphora and started living in tribe.

The father of bride is a learned man, the man who actually worked to raise the family after all stress - so his eyes are sharper than **باز** - Eagle, he knows the birds by their beak. So Zipproh's father Jethro saw a believer in Moses and handed his beloved daughter to a noble man.

The intelligence and reason of Moses were as complimenting as Zipphora's cunning and humouristic nature. This marriage had bring new peace and joy in the consciousness of tribe.

---

Suddenly the dreams started turned into reality. To accept, call, believe and prayer was Moses way on the journey to God, leave the freewill to the destiny, because "God shall be our saviour", For which God started revealing to Moses. 

God called Moses to mountain Tur - **کوه طور** to reveal him his prophet-hood duties that after 150-200 years of imprisonment of Hebrews, Moses now shall get responsibility to set them straight into the right direction.

Moses discovered he had a brother named Aaron (**هارون**) whom will be his guide in the city of Per-Ramesses. Where in the centre of universe - Pharoah and Moses fought over. 

The Pharoah's activities were reigned by magic, control and deceit. While the good Hebrews were faithful and loyal to God. Moses, He guided his people on the right track. While Pharoah was scheming and making plans to defeat Moses, Moses only were counselling for the betterment of all and his people to freedom.

But every time after punishment from God, Pharoah's heart turned as soft as candle wax to God's fire. But every time as he tried to act good, the devil in charge used to trap him in his superiority and ego. The man who do not listen to god is destined to destruction and death. The person who cannot speak has better chances than one who cannot listen. 

---

The ego of  Pharoah ate Pharoah's ears. The counsel of Moses threatened the ego of Pharoah and superior idol Pharoah made of himself. The stone hearted Pharoah was unable to untangle and enjoy his life. 

This stone became the cause of his death, upon the defeat of ego multiple times by God's decree and Prophet Moses - Pharoah endured a deserving death.

The example of leadership under God was established. That one who is slave to God is the king of world.

Hebrews got freedom under Prophet Moses leadership. However Hebrews were still used to their disbelieving - agonistic patterns of the old leadership under Pharoah, while Moses and Aaron were their granted guides and they used to complain about food, shelter and many requests, they were ungrateful. For which still Moses used to pray to God to get them necessities as they demanded. 

From there, the organisation, systematic approach - the plan of planning was born. Moses and Aaron set the god commandments as laws to govern their people.

But people were cunning still rotten to the core, because Egyptians harsh treatment of Hebrews made Hebrews forget about love and God.

So as Prophet Moses went to mountain to get a revelation from God, the people were furious at Prophet Moses absence and out of pity displaying their real behaviour of materialism, started worshipping the Golden calf.

---

While worshipping an idol, they lost their eyes to the shinning skin of the Golden calf. It was worst sight for Prophet Moses to see. But God did not allow Moses to interfere for while because God had justice in mind and his reveal his power again. While they were worshipping - Hebrews the idol of a calf - the tribulation started increasing day by day until the day they asked to repent.

The God commanded Moses to guide those people to find a specific type of a cow to sacrifice. To break the idol, God demanded such a sacrifice because idol worshipping and egoism only could lead to annihilation of the self.

Therefore God revealed to people that there is no superiority of anyone in the God's eyes except believers of one almighty God.
