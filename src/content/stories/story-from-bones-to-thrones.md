> #### Once there in a jungle, there was lions and there was dogs, it’s wild how instead of deadly hyenas, there was dogs, how funny in a strange world we have put dogs which are domestic animals put up against the lions.

> ### PLOT

All other animals were prey to these dogs and lions. We have seen lions killing dogs 10 times more than them. Lions were proud, fiery, and powerful animals, almost 2.5 times bigger than the dogs. Because of that power, most of the prey had been killed by lions for food.

The dogs, being more numerous, were unable to feed their population. Dogs also needed more space to live in the jungle. They had to stay in tiny caves, where five dogs would fit into the space meant for three. They were living like peasants.

As lions were powerful, they were also kind—or one could say satirical—as they used to throw some meaty bones to the dogs. This allowed the dog population to survive and ensured that lions remained the kings of the jungle. There was a duopoly to prevent a monopoly. Without competition, lions would not be what they are; without fights, a warrior wouldn’t cease to exist. Lions understood this subconsciously, so that was the course for a long time in the jungle.

> ### STORY - PROBLEM

But as the time came, the drought happened. There was limited water, so all the animals had to carefully use water. Without water, even the lions were not enjoying the feast. Reproduction of animals stopped for 6–7 months. With limited food resources and wanting to maintain a healthy supply chain, the lions understood this, so they stopped killing prey.

Since the dogs were already down to the bones, they needed food. All the extra prey the lions had saved for the reproduction of animals when the drought would resolve, the dogs started killing—also taking some young animal kids. 

As soon as the lions found out about this, they stopped the dogs, which issued a war notice from the lions to the dogs. The dogs, being smart and much healthier now, accepted it. As a result, some deaths happened. Although the lions were weak, they were still able to handle the dogs, but sadly, one lion died, whereas there were 10 dog deaths. This episode was really a bloodbath.

The dogs returned to their caves for a union. In front of the Dog King, who is a monstrously big dog, he said, "They have always treated us badly and made us survive on bones. This time, with so many casualties, how could we win? They are almost three times more powerful."

> ### Unexpected Plan

So the day comes. The dog king ordered 20 dogs to go attack the overall lion kingdom of 200 lions. Twenty dogs went, and the lions killed them all easily. Then they went for a rest. The lions chitchatted, "What kind of stupid dogs these are. They are so many in numbers, yet they sent only 20 dogs."

Days passed by, and again, 20 dogs came to attack the lions. Again, the lions killed these 20 dogs. The same happened one more time, and the lions again killed 20 dogs. As a result, the lions lowered their barriers and protection as they got lazy. The lions were unaware of what was coming.

In the next attack, unsuspectedly, there were not 20 dogs but 100 dogs, causing the lions 76 casualties. Lions now became aware of the problem but knew their fate. The dogs, now confident, sent 200 dogs, and they took over the whole lion kingdom and killed everyone.

Dogs officially took over the jungle. Some young lions were set free and not killed. Dogs are not hyenas, but now the situation is the other way around. Dogs now throw bones to the lions.
