## *As always inspired by ਮੁਰਸ਼ਿਦ ਰੂਮੀ - مولانا رومی - Maulana Rumi*

### CONTEXT

The story sets when the modernisation and leisure seemed to appear to the rich class of merchants. Many of these merchants were very noble people who had worked hard for centuries of generations. 

Many noble merchants were busy in trade until the entertainment appeared in the forms of Circuses and even silent films, even theatrical play and the operas were quite famous in those times. 

So there was this special merchant who became so intoxicated with devil's wine that this merchant used to go to one circus after every two days. First the intoxication of that circus was only taken as leisure on weekends, then weekends turned into weekdays and it became everyday affair to go to watch two shows of circus every day. And as rich as the merchant was, he was spending all his money on buying Circus tickets. 

The reason for intoxication was the trickery of the clown in the circus who had a special talent that made that clown famous and influential of all times that only the special wealthy merchants used to go. The clown was so rainbow and gay, always happy like sunflowers in summer.

But that summer was only display, actually the death of winter was that clown's businesses. Often buying people's laughter cheaply by tormenting and torturing his woes in the play of circus. Whilst the clown's devil plotted and targeted woes by the guile of words and other trickery to appear himself stupid, but behind that mask he used to smirk like devil as he know he is making everybody stupid.

Under the devil's forbidden fruit of pleasures, the devil laid the trap, Similarly this clown he set up the circus, and made the audience intoxicated with laughter and escapes the circus tricks. 

### BEGINNINGS

Immediately you're on a journey of correct action, but you are keep failing in devil's trap again and again such was these merchants journey all along whilst they promised to not waste money on circus, they kept going back for an easy enjoyment and laughter.

Because that cursed devil has gained the upper hand over Adam **ادم** whilst the wise god has always been merciful and welcoming despite our shortcomings.

At same time, cunning devil is scheming to  have more pleasure, the cursed devil is all about circus and in the form of circus, the rich merchant of a man becomes poor.

Because not only the special merchant traded his time for growth to watch circus, but also wasted the resources such as money and health, moreover also traded himself for the addiction of circus.

The wicked addicted merchant only thought of himself and his addiction, which is the path of devil's way. 

The devil in the form of clown was the circus organizer and the commander of props of woes to create an act, that act of giving commands to woes and sees woes failing was the laughter of audience.

The cunning of that cursed clown was it could see the teeth of people coming in the circus and understand who sells their laughter cheaply. Whom was new to the game of circus. The clown was the best private entertainer. 

He tricked the people whom were new by engaging with them giving them special treatment. Because the devil of clown knew the rest of people were already sold.

On the surface, this clown seems having fun but under his mask, that clown was alone - depressed - a child of devil whom was abandoned by god because of his pride and ego that he is superior.

![unsplash](/images/stories-assets/unsplash%20(8).jpeg)

To escape the pain and continuing his devilish ways - he made plot for the descendants of Adam to make them fail.

Then that special merchant became poor - broke after spending money on the circus of evil clown.  The merchant son of wise man, whom also saw the same circus but instead of going broke and gambling, that merchant's wise father chose to make turn to Temple/Church and pleaded to his home - the godly soul.

But this foolish merchant even though he was special had abandoned his will and everything to Evil clown's circus and now that same clown was laughing at the broke merchant.

So having no refugee, since the merchant had forgotten god, turned to men for assistance and even God knowing men would not help the irresponsible merchant. 

> "Beside bread and water, what can a common righteous man could provide to homeless merchant? Oh reader everything comes from God's provisions, If he says the wind to make somebody honey and gives the voice of nightingale **(بل بل)** or if he makes somebody wolf (**گرگ**) and sharp teeth"

So same clown one day offered the merchant to come work in the circus, not out of pity or sympathy, but it was a trap. Same way the devil traps people by breaking them first by forbidden pleasure - later only to make them a slave - **غلام** . As cursed Devil always tried to gain an upper hand over god and started controlling human's freewill. Devil thought by increasing circus of pleasures, he was gaining control over earth.

> #### *"And you did not throw when you threw, but it was Allah who threw."* 

But devil doesn't know God's plan as neither as we whom are not purified as our father of mankind Adam. "Have Devil forgotten who created him as everything?"

The poor person of merchant started working for the clown. Clown's words was "Oh I shall be hands and you shall be my puppet, you shall dance on my hands, geggegegge"

Merchant understood the clown's master plan moment by moment (**دم به دم**), whilst clown used to  beat up merchant as woes in the circus, he was paying merchant just enough for survival. The clown was beating him - tormenting him mercilessly. 

Until the Merchant started to say "Alas, Alas (**لا حول, لا حول) سوزاندن** - burning on my ass"… Merchant  pleaded and cried and remembered God, God started showing the cunning of devil's end if Merchant keeps working in circus.

"Just Surrender" was God's counsel to Merchant, God revealed - commanded Merchant to surrender, to just surrender and God shall be merchant's saviour.

But Merchant had doubts in his heart, he was still not a true believer. He deemed God superficial and the invention of mind. While the god revealed the way, the poor merchant only saw the thoughts - he didn't held God's light, he didn't see the glow - all that blind one was seeing his thoughts.

As God used to reveal this poor merchant the way everyday. Everyday Devil of clown used to make him work of deceit for many hours of circus - until Merchant was drained and broken. Devilish clown didn't give merchant or any woes any break.

Everyday Devilish clown used to wake merchant from Godly sleep of prayers and lamentation. Merchant would wake up and becomes terrified of clown's devilish real face.

But clown was the master of deceit and sugar coated words that he would only treat this merchant kindly and used to offer merchant some sugar laced tea, the sugar of tea has an intoxicated taste that made merchant forgot devil of clown's real face. 

While intoxicated, the merchant used to laugh while performing for first few hours until hangover whilst also chasing new clowns in the audience. As intoxication used to wear down, the merchant used to weep silently, as to remember the devil's master plan of choosing him (the merchant) in the audience. Remembering how the same clown was making him laugh treating him special to make him spend all his money.

Now the clown of devil is founding new youth, however the clown team of woes - the props were diverse - Merchant saw not only himself as youth but there were some mothers (**مادرها**) and fathers (**پدرها**). 

God was always asking merchant during his prayers "oh **پدر** - father when shall you learn and surrender this devil's route ?"

God always promised the merchant to show him the new way, a new life as Merchant abandons the devil clown's way.

Until Merchant saw and recognised that devil used whole of merchant's body and now whipping marks were all over his body and day by day the pain was just increasing.

> "Until what days would you eat sugar of devil, whom always beat and break you, only offering you sweets and gluttony to keep going - performing in circus?"

The merchant checked his neighbours in the small society of clown woes - team, where that devil used to charge them for rent and food as much as he used to pay - that clown.

The merchant saw these mothers and fathers adopted the ways of diseases to not face the way of god only to eat devil's sugar and sometimes pizza on appreciation days which made these neighbours sick to the gut.

Instead of medicine for sick, they were eating food which were making them sick and stealing their youth. The merchant got afraid and terrified of their conditions and saw his destiny to be similar. Until one day, he chose to not take devilish's clowns sugary tea.

The devilish clown put that merchant into circus show that day, clown whipped him in front of audience and audience laughed. The devil saw the merchant wailing - the clown got amazed (**حیران**) and schemed a new plot.

Now that clown planned to give merchant a sweeter, better candy instead of that cheap sugary tea. The tragedy was that greedy merchant again falling for devil's scheme. Now that Candy (**کندی** **حشیش**) gave merchant even more intoxication for longer hours.

However when merchant got sober from that candy, he again realized the reality to be only more worsening and more painful than before.

That clown was devilish to the point - that instead of sleeping - he used to plan only next day circus - that's how he made a huge circus empire as big and powerful.

And like that merchant, he trapped many of millions of this world in circus as he had a keen evil eye to recognize who was obeying and one who was rebellious. However the obeying ones were only destined for more torture and degradation as whom they were listening to - the devil.

So this devil clown made classes of people of merchants he managed to trap. The most obeying were given more processed cheap artificial sugar in the tea to not only make them physically sick but also break them psychologically and spiritually. 

The rebellious ones were rather more treated well to keep them in trap, "oh if devil is treating you well - giving candy probably healthy and organic - still don't take it at any costs".

The rebellious ones were God's favourites whom always asked god for help, and God showed them mercy.

After failing multiple times, that rebellious merchant decided to not take anything from clown - sugar to any forbidden fruit and still used to take devil's painful whipping in the show.

That pain without sugar - instead of causing burning and inflammation - used to run that rebellious one's blood run hot. 

That rebellious one of merchant starting thanking god (**شُکر**) for all his strength which led him to way of challenging and checkmating (**مات**) devil.

The accursed devil was getting weak in his escapes because whipping of devilish clown now was not making the merchant jump in terror and now because of sobriety - the pretentious laugh was not present for the audience's entertainment. 

The audience instead of now laughing, they started to weep. When that wicked merchant shaped woe turned into Godly human. When the devil whipped him, he used to weep on the stage and the audience used to lament.

The laughter of crooked turned into the symmetry of teary eyes. The audience saw the god in human who didn't eat devil's sugar. 

Now because of his rebelliousness - devilish clown kicked the merchant out of circus, his rented house. Suddenly after that audience in circus started disappearing.

Because now instead of clown's laughter, audience of that weeping merchant was more present - they wanted to see that godly soul clay-less merchant - instead of that hard skinned cunning devilish clown. 

So clown realizing his mistake, now faced silence and his own demons where there was not any entertainment and fun anymore. When the noise in circus stopped, clown found merchant on the streets and called him again. 

The merchant whom had become Sufi - Dervish (**فقیر**) and content with his life. He chose the path of God. To be content in what God had given him, the way of godly revelations in the path, the skull of limbs and some intellect (**عقل**).

The devilish clown came begging to that glow of man - Sufi turned from wicked merchant to come back to circus but that Sufi declined. 

Devil started telling him the story of his failure and weeping of Sufi's magic on audience and promised Sufi the best of luxury could buy and afford. But Sufi declined again as the wrong doing of the devilish clown.

Because the higher level of escape devil would provide - It only has one purpose - to only make men forget that there is a God and reaffirming that the devil is God.

But that proud Sufi full of his faith (**دین**), said no to devilish clown as to him the poverty of God was more valuable than the possessions of 7 worlds. He demanded and manifested only one thing that was God.

> #### "Imagine Oh readers, what is that state of Sufi, besides **الله** - God he doesn't know or value anything"

Devil of the clown saw such a light, that he got burned and became helpless. God came at such stage from heavens and appeared to the clown and Sufi with same message, the state of tranquility. 

The light of Sufi's eyes made clownish devil tapped into that tranquility. God said "oh men of soul and of fire, Didn't I tell you ", ***"And you did not throw when you threw, but it was Allah who threw.,"****"*

 The devilish clown felt the energy to his core and cried inside like 100 hell were set upon him for millions of years. To the people on outside, the men of society whom were observing Sufi and Clown from far. It seemed few minutes they were gone for. 

But in reality, God showed themselves millions of years, beyond time and eternity, they seemed to become obsolete for God knows how many years.

Only later God commanded Sufi to follow the devilish clown to the circus because the treasure was buried under the ruins of autumn to experience death in deadly winter, only to come back alive in summer. 

Sufi obeyed God and went to circus with clown, they prepared for the show and went on stage in the front of audience.

But the weird thing this time was the clown was not laughing but he was crying and weeping as Sufi was laughing taking whips. The audience was shocked.

So in the end, everyone got the message that the man of God had won over that cursed devil. 

After that - that devilish clown also chose the faith and stopped tricking people. From the cunning clown - he became the justly king who banned circuses and tricking people. 

As the crookedness of cheap laughter in the world was replaced  by genuine smile of God's clemency (**سکون**).

> "Oh readers aren't we all merchants (**سوداگران**) selling to each others? Isn't it a wonderful world?"
